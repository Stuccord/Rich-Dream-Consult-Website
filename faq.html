Travel & Visa Services

We process visa applications for the USA, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, Schengen area (Europe), and all other major destinations. Our experienced team guides you through every step of the application process to maximise your approval chances.
Processing times vary by destination and visa type. Typically, tourist visas take 5–15 business days, while business and student visas can take 4–8 weeks. We always advise applying well in advance. Contact us and we'll give you a precise estimate for your specific case.
No consultancy can legally guarantee visa approval, as the final decision rests with the respective embassy or consulate. However, our proven track record and thorough preparation of documentation significantly increases your chances of success. We have a high success rate backed by multiple ABMA awards.
Requirements vary by destination but generally include: a valid passport (6+ months validity), completed application form, passport photos, bank statements (3–6 months), proof of accommodation, flight itinerary, travel insurance, and a cover letter. Our team will give you a tailored checklist for your specific application.
Yes! We assist with new passport applications, renewals, lost passport replacement, and express processing where available. We also help with birth certificates, marriage certificates, and other supporting documents through the appropriate Ghanaian government agencies.
