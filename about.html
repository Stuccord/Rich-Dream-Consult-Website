The Evolution

Established on January 1, 2020, as Rich Dreams Business, we embarked on a mission to redefine consultancy in Ghana. On October 19, 2022, we rebranded to Rich Dream Consult to reflect our expanding global footprint and diverse service offerings.

Today, we are a multi-award-winning firm, recognized for our excellence in Information Technology, Travel, and Business Management. Our head office remains a beacon of innovation in Teshie–Accra, with active operations in Kumasi.
